Feature 01

Real-Time Metering

Granular visibility into every circuit, panel, and load. Our smart meters capture consumption data at sub-second intervals, feeding live dashboards that expose waste, anomalies, and optimization opportunities the moment they appear.

  • Circuit-level monitoring with per-device energy attribution
  • Live dashboards with customizable views, alerts, and historical comparison
  • Anomaly detection that flags unusual consumption patterns automatically
Feature 02

AI Load Optimization

Machine learning models analyze your consumption patterns, occupancy data, and tariff structures to automatically shift, reduce, and schedule loads for maximum savings. The system gets smarter every day, learning from every watt consumed.

  • Peak shaving that automatically reduces demand during high-tariff periods
  • Demand response integration for utility incentive programs
  • Predictive scheduling that pre-conditions spaces during off-peak hours
Feature 03

Solar and Renewable Integration

Seamlessly integrate solar arrays, wind generation, and battery storage into a unified energy management platform. Track generation versus consumption in real time, optimize self-consumption, and manage grid export for maximum return.

  • Real-time generation tracking with performance ratio monitoring
  • Intelligent grid synchronization managing export and import optimization
  • Battery storage management with charge and discharge scheduling
Feature 04

HVAC Energy Management

HVAC typically accounts for 40-60% of a building's energy consumption. Our zone-level optimization ensures every unit operates at peak efficiency, responding dynamically to occupancy patterns, weather conditions, and thermal load.

  • Zone-level optimization with independent temperature and airflow control
  • Occupancy-based control that reduces cooling and heating in unoccupied zones
  • Efficiency scoring and benchmarking against industry standards

The Ecosystem

Hardware That Powers Intelligence

Industrial-grade meters, monitors, and controllers engineered for accuracy, reliability, and seamless integration into any electrical infrastructure.

Smart Energy Meter

Bi-directional energy meter with sub-second sampling, power quality analysis, and harmonics detection. RS485, Modbus, and Wi-Fi connectivity. DIN-rail mount.

Power Monitor

Multi-circuit power monitor supporting up to 42 channels. Current transformers included. Real-time voltage, current, power factor, and energy for every circuit.

Solar Monitor

Dedicated solar inverter monitor tracking generation, performance ratio, string-level analysis, and grid export. Supports all major inverter brands via Modbus.

HVAC Controller

BACnet and Modbus compatible HVAC controller with zone-level temperature management. Integrates with VRF, chiller, and split AC systems for unified control.

Lighting Controller

DALI-2 and 0-10V dimming controller with daylight harvesting, occupancy integration, and scene scheduling. Reduces lighting energy by up to 60%.

Carbon Analytics Software

Cloud platform for carbon footprint calculation, Scope 1, 2, and 3 tracking, ESG reporting, and sustainability benchmarking. API-first architecture.
