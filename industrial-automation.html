Industrial Automation

Factory Intelligence.
Predictive. Connected.

From the factory floor to the control room. One intelligent platform monitoring, predicting, and protecting every asset in your operation.

01

Predictive Maintenance

Machine learning models analyze vibration patterns, thermal signatures, and acoustic data in real time. Predict failures days before they happen, schedule maintenance during planned downtime, and eliminate unplanned stoppages that cost thousands per hour.

Vibration pattern anomaly detection
Thermal drift and bearing wear alerts
Remaining useful life (RUL) estimation
Automated work order generation
02

SCADA & Cloud Monitoring

Unified SCADA dashboards accessible from anywhere. Real-time process visualization, historical trend analysis, and automated reporting. Bridge legacy PLCs with cloud-native analytics through our protocol adapters supporting Modbus, OPC-UA, and BACnet.

Modbus, OPC-UA, BACnet protocol support
Multi-site centralized monitoring
Automated shift and compliance reports
Role-based access and audit trails
03

Warehouse Intelligence

LoRaWAN-powered asset tracking across thousands of square meters. Know the exact location of every pallet, tool, and vehicle. Optimize storage layout, monitor cold chain integrity, and automate inventory audits with real-time precision.

Real-time indoor asset positioning
Cold chain temperature compliance
Forklift and AGV fleet management
Automated cycle count and shrinkage alerts
04

Safety Video Analytics

AI-powered cameras detect PPE violations, restricted zone breaches, and unsafe behavior in real time. Automatic alerts to supervisors, visual compliance dashboards, and incident documentation for regulatory audits. Safety is no longer reactive.

PPE detection (helmet, vest, gloves, goggles)
Restricted zone intrusion alerts
Fall detection and man-down monitoring
Compliance scoring and trend reports
