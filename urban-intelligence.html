Urban Intelligence

Smart City Solutions
for Tomorrow

Intelligent infrastructure that senses, adapts, and optimizes. From streetlights to traffic flows, one platform for the urban future.

01

Smart Street Lighting

Adaptive LED streetlights that respond to ambient conditions, pedestrian presence, and traffic density. Each luminaire communicates wirelessly, dimming during quiet hours and brightening for safety events. Centralized dashboards monitor thousands of nodes, tracking energy consumption and automating maintenance alerts across the entire city grid.

Adaptive Brightness Energy Savings Fault Detection

A city that dims its lights when no one is watching and brightens the moment you need it

02

Traffic Management & ANPR

AI-powered traffic signal optimization that reads real-time congestion patterns and adjusts phase timings dynamically. Integrated Automatic Number Plate Recognition cameras enable instant vehicle identification for security, toll management, and parking enforcement. The system learns from historical data, predicting bottlenecks before they form.

Real-Time Flow Number Plate Recognition Congestion Prediction
“

Traffic that flows like water because the intersections already know what is coming

03

Surveillance & Video Analytics

Edge-AI cameras that go beyond recording. Real-time behavior analytics detect intrusions, crowd anomalies, and safety incidents within milliseconds. Face recognition, object tracking, and heat mapping transform passive footage into actionable intelligence. Integrated command centers provide city-wide situational awareness with automated alert escalation.

AI-Powered Monitoring Behavior Detection Crowd Analysis
“

Not just cameras watching. Intelligence that understands what it sees

04

Environmental Monitoring

Dense networks of LoRaWAN sensors tracking air quality, noise levels, temperature, humidity, and particulate matter across every neighborhood. Real-time environmental dashboards enable data-driven policy decisions. Automated alerts trigger when pollution thresholds are exceeded, while long-term trend analysis guides urban planning for healthier, more liveable cities.

The Intelligence

01

Edge Computing

Data processing happens at the source. Each sensor node carries edge intelligence, reducing latency to milliseconds and eliminating dependency on cloud connectivity for critical decisions. Street-level responses happen in real time.

02

Unified Command Center

A single dashboard for the entire city. Multi-layer GIS mapping, real-time incident management, and cross-system correlation. When a traffic incident occurs, street lighting, surveillance, and emergency services coordinate automatically.

03

Scalable Architecture

Start with a pilot zone and scale to city-wide deployment. LoRaWAN mesh networks support 10,000+ nodes per gateway. Open APIs integrate with existing municipal systems, legacy infrastructure, and third-party platforms seamlessly.
